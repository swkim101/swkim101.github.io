I'm a Ph. D. student @ Purdue CS advised by Prof. Dave (Jing) Tian and Prof. Dongyan Xu working at PurSec Lab and FRIENDS .

My research focuses on developing systematic methods to improve the security of operating systems, which form the foundation of computing applications. I am interested in a broad range of system security topics, including automated vulnerability discovery, exploitation prevention, and bug replication.
